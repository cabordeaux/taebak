PARSONS BRINCKERHOFF SELECTS TRANSAMERICAN ENGINEERS TO PERFORM CONSTRUCTION STAKING ON YERBA BUENA ISLAND (YBI) I-80 EAST-SIDE RAMPS PROJECT

The Yerba Buena Island (YBI) I-80 East-Side Ramps project will relocate the westbound exit ramp on the north side of I-80 and reconstruct the westbound entrance ramp on the north side of I-80 onto Bay Bridge. The project will improve traffic safety, geometric design, and traffic operations of the ramps. The total estimated project cost is currently $98.04 million. The project is on schedule for completion and ribbon cutting in August 2016.
